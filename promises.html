Our promises

Our promises attempt to include needs of students but more importantly to eliminate the elitist group, French Immersion. Here's a list of our promises:

    Left-handed accessories for left-handed students.
    Add an extra elevator to prevent conflicts if multiple wheelchairs had to enter.
    Classrooms for students with special needs.
    Eliminate French Immersion and extend it as an activity for ASAP as well as other languages.
    After-school tutoring sessions.
    Wider lockers.
    Bags for everyone to carry their stuff in as this would allow better organization.
    Dictonaries and/or Therasuses on every desk in classes like ELA or Social Studies.
