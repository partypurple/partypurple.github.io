About the Purple Party

The Purple Party attempts to meet all of your needs that the school fails to meet, this inludes but limited to: left-handed accessories, wider lockers, after-school tutoring sessions, etc.

We'd also like to eliminate French Immersion as we feel it's too elitist and it's better off being an activity for ASAP.

If you feel that the school should be more accessible with your needs, please vote for the Purple Party!
